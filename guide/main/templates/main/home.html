Welcome!

This guide has been made for children and young people in care to help answers questions you may have about being in care, such as:

  • How did I become looked after?
  • Who can I talk to about problems I'm having?
  • What do these words or acronyms mean?

The guide will have advice about your health and wellbeing, and it will also show you where to find help with your money and life skills like cooking and laundry.

It will also have advice about what to do next, if you're older.
