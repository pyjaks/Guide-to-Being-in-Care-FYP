Your Rights

  • You have a right to an education.
  • You have a right to be heard and have a say about issues affecting you.
  • You have a right to practice your religion.
  • You have the right to have your care plan reviewed.
  • You have a right to be protected from violence, abuse and neglect.
  • You have a right to have your identity, religious and cultural needs met.
  • You have a right to family life and to have contact as long as it is safe for you.
  • You have a right to be treated in a non-discriminatory way.
